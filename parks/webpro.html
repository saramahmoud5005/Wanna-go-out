Cairo

Al-Azhar Park

Information :

  • Al-Azhar Park is considered one of the most beautiful and famous gardens of Cairo Governorate, but it is also one of the largest parks in Cairo.
  • The park includes a number of different attractions that are suitable for large and small for entertainment and viewing the landscapes.

Location : Cairo governorate, specifically in the ancient Fatimid city area.

Ticket price : On normal weekdays, entrance tickets for an adult are 5 pounds, but on public holidays and Friday, 7 pounds. For children under the age of 18, the ticket price is three and a half pounds.

Visiting times : From 9Am to 10Pm

Aquarium

Information :

  • Established by Khedive Ismail in the year 1867 .
  • Designed in the form of fish gills by French experts with Egyptian resources such as mud and red sand .

Location : Zamalek on Gabalaya Street in Cairo

Ticket price :5 pounds for Egyptian & 20 pounds for Foreigners and free for Children under the age of four .

Visiting times : From 9.30Am to 5Pm except for Tuesday

Al Andalus Garden

Information :

  • It was established in the mid-thirties of the last century during the reign of King Fouad the First, who allocated two acres to it on the banks of the Nile.
  • Designed in the Andalusian style, which expresses the civilization of Muslims in Spain during their rule, and is included in the list of Islamic and Coptic monuments.

Location : in the Zamalek area in Giza, across from the Cairo Opera House, and overlooks the Nile River.

Ticket price :- The price of the entrance ticket to Andalus Park for Egyptians of all ages is only 5 pounds, and the amount doubles for foreigners
-An additional sum of 50 pounds will be added to the ticket price, in case the visitor wants to take a photography camera with him inside.

Visiting times : From 9Am to 10Pm

Japanese garden

Information :

  • It is a public park established by the architect Zulfiqar Pasha 1919 in the Asian style to symbolize the civilizations of the East.
  • The park contains some statues that symbolize different historical eras, such as the lotus flower that carries the statue of Zulfiqar Pasha.

Location : Helwan-Mostafa El Maraghi Street

Ticket price :Do not exceed 10 pounds per person.

Visiting times : From 9Am to 10Pm

Giza

Giza Zoo

Information :

  • It is the largest zoo in Egypt and the Middle East, and the first and oldest zoo in Africa .
  • It was called "the crown jewel of African zoos".

Location : Giza-Charles de Gaulle .

Ticket price :The price of an entrance ticket to the zoo is estimated at 65 pounds on weekdays, with the exception of the Tuesday on which the entrance ticket was raised to 65 pounds.

Visiting times : From 9 am to 5 pm every day, except for Tuesday.

Safari park

Information :

  • It is the second park to be found in Egypt after Al-Azhar Park.
  • It has been built in a rustic style on an area of about 40 acres

Location : Imbaba Airport - below the Dari Road - next to the new Ahmed Orabi axis

Ticket price :10 pounds per person

Visiting times : From 9Am to 11Pm

Alexandria

Waterfalls garden

Information :

  • It occupies an area of 8 acres and is characterized by terraces of different heights, as well as artificial lakes and artificial waterfalls.
  • By the remains of the towers and wall of Alexandria. It also has underground tanks .

Location : It is located in the Latin neighborhood, near Bab Sharq .

Ticket price : For free

Visiting times : From 10Am to 12Pm

Alexandria International Park

Information :

  • It was established in 1987.
  • It contains plants and trees, and there are many wonderful water games in addition to a part dedicated to the amusement park

Location : 6 Alexandria Desert Road, Abis, Muharram Bey, Alexandria

Ticket price :10 pounds per person
The ticket price may reach about 40 pounds if there are theatrical performances held on the park's grounds

Visiting times : From 9Am to 6Pm

Dakahlia

Rose Island

Information :

  • It is a hundred and twenty kilometers from the capital, Cairo
  • It is the same city of Mansoura, which is located in the Arab Republic of Egypt, specifically on the eastern bank of the Damietta River

Location : Mansoura

Ticket price :3 pounds per person

Visiting times : From 9Am to 6Pm

Mansoura animal park

Information :

  • Mansoura Zoo is the second oldest park in Egypt, after Giza Park.
  • It was donated by Al-Khawaja «Toreel» who established the neighborhood in which it is located, and concluded a usufruct contract, stipulating that it be a zoo only.

Location : Ali Mubarak, Mansoura (Section 2), Mansoura, Dakahlia

Ticket price :65 pounds per person

Visiting times : From 9Am to 7Pm
